Brand Identity — Citrus Co.

Category: Branding / Packaging

A complete brand identity created for Citrus Co., a craft beverage startup. Deliverables included logo design, stationery set, and packaging design for their signature juice line.

Design Approach

The visual identity was inspired by fresh fruit, clean typography, and a vibrant color palette. The design focused on approachability while maintaining a premium feel.

Deliverables

  • Logo design & color system
  • Stationery templates (business card, letterhead)
  • Packaging concepts for bottles and cans
